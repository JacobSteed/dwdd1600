About Us

Who are We?

We are Mass Rez, tasked with resurecting face-to-face gaming.

With the widespread use of online technology, gaming especially has changed within the last 10 years. Before Xbox Live, or the PlayStation online network, console, and PC gamers could only play with friends using LAN. Nowadays, online capabilities have affected LAN parties, mostly losing the face-to-face fun of gaming with friends. Our goul is to keep the face-to-face gaming scene alive, providing tools and information of what is necessary to host a LAN party.

What is LAN?

LAN, or Local Area Network is an internet connection that is mainly wired, and is condensed to a local houshold, school, building etc. A LAN connection has been used to connect gaming consoles or computers for the better part of the 90's through the 2000's.

Learn more here

What is Required for a LAN Connection?

  • More than one console or PC
  • Ethernet Cables
  • A network switch
  • Access to the local router

Steps to Connect

  1. Access your local internet router
  2. Connect an ethernet cable to the router.
  3. Connect the other end of the cable to a switch.
  4. Connect as many cables to the ports of the switch to the number of people.
  5. Finally, connect a cable to each gaming system.

Depending on the ammount of people, you might need more cables and switches.
